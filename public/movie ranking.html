The Best Movies According to Pooja

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Train To Busan

I liked this movie very much. Although it is an horrifying zombie movie it is filled with courage, father's love. The ending is so heart touching.

Karate Kid

Its one of the movie which I can watch without any number of times. The movie talks about how we can achieve our goal through hardwork, practice, patience and perseverance. I like all the characters in this movie.

MeleParambil Aanveed

I like this movie. I like the locations of this movie. It is a family movie combined with romance and comedy.
